Setting Up Your App

  • Provide Access to your location details
  • Provide Access to your phone contacts
  • Update emmergency contacts list

How to use you app during an emergency situation

  • Open your app
  • Shake your phone or press the alert button
  • It will notify your emergency contacts with your current location
  • It Also rings the alarm if alarm is On
  • This process will be repeated untill you shake your phone or press the alert button again
  • Long press on the alert button to call 911
